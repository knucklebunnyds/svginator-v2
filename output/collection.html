KBDS Doge Knuckleheads

Welcome to the first-ever interactive DOGE inscription collection of its kind! The KBDS Doge Knuckleheads represent a groundbreaking fusion of blockchain technology and dynamic SVG artistry.

  • Total Supply: 300 Unique Knuckleheads
  • Format: Interactive SVG with Dynamic Theming
  • Traits: 8 Categories with Multiple Variations

Revolutionary Features

  • First Ever Custom Coloring: Holders can personalize their NFT's colors - a groundbreaking feature never before seen in Web3 on any blockchain

  • Fully on-chain SVG artwork
  • Animated background options
  • Semantic SVG structure for developer flexibility

Each Knucklehead is a unique combination of traits, with some featuring rare animated backgrounds and special attributes. As the first collection to offer custom coloring capabilities to holders, KBDS Doge Knuckleheads sets a new standard for interactive NFTs in the Web3 space.
